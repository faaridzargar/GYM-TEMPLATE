About Us

Faarid Zargar was founded in 2023 by Faarid Zargar, a talented artist with a passion for manga. Faarid has been drawing manga since he was a child, and his drawings have been featured in magazines and books around the world.

We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality manga drawings. Our drawings are made with high-quality materials and are carefully crafted by our team of experienced artists.
